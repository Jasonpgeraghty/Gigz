GigZ
What is GigZ? GigZ allows local entertainers and local venues to get in contact with each other. Each user creates their own profile to be able to provide key information about themselves such as preferred genre, mobile number, email and bio increase their appeal. It provides a stream line process of making bookings directly to their desired party without having to go through a third party. The application has many useful features to ensure you know exactly what you are booking, such as a review system to provide unfiltered past experiences. It also has a favouriting system to allows users an easy approach of rebooking venues or entertainers they enjoyed in the past. All this is absolutely free!
