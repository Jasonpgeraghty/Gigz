What is GigZ? For bands:

GigZ allows entertainers to instantly contact a range of venues to perform in. You can then send a request to perform!


For venues:

GigZ provides venues with a wide variety of entertainers to perform in their venue. A erquest can then be sent to the entertainer to get them to perform! Feel free to contact us: info@gigz.com Login Sign Up!
